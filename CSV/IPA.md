##Diacritics
* [ː]	Long
* [ˑ]	Half-long
* [ˈ]	Primasry Stress
* [ˌ]	Secondary Stress
* [w]	Non-syllabic "U"
* [\_␣]	Dental
* [\_+]	Advanced
* [\_-]	Retracted
* [\_▴]	Raised
* [\_▾]	Lowered
* [\_ˌ]	Syllabics
* [\_o]	Voiceless
* [\_v]	Voiced
* [\^⌝]	Silent Release
* [\^"]	Centeralized
* [\^h]	Aspirated
* [\^ɦ]	Breathy Voiced
* [\^j]	Palatalized
* [\^u]	Extra-short
* [\^w]	Labialized
* [\^ʕ]	Velarized
